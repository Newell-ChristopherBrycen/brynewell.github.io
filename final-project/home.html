San Diego Temple

The San Diego Temple was first announced on April 7th of 1984. It wasn't until 4 years later, on February 27th of 1988 that the ground was broken for this temple. It was dedicated on April 25th of 1993. The dedicatory prayer can be viewed on lds.org by clicking here. The San Diego Temple is truly a testament to the beauty of God's creation and mans ability to bring that beauty to life in its architecture.
